chapter 8
Appointment of Auditors, Bankers and Legal Adiviser

....

Learn more

The Association shall appoint auditors compromising of three members who are not members of the executive to audit the account of the body at the last meeting of every year.

Such auditors shall have recourse to financial records and other documents necessary for the execution of their obligations.

The auditors shall make its findings known to the association within three months.

The Association shall examine such findings and move a resolution for its rejection, amendment or adoption.

The Association shall have an account with a reputable Bank. The President, Financial Secretary and Treasurer shall serve as the signatories.

The members in a General Meeting shall appoint a Legal adviser of the body to offer necessary legal advice to the Association as at when due to ensure
strict compliance to the relevant laws of the land.


The idea of the Alumni Association was conceived when it was considered necessary to create an organization that would embrace different generations of the University. The body was expected to play a dominant role in the life of the University by reflecting the views of its graduates.
